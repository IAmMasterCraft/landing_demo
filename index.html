INTRODUCTION

From my experience in the Jewellery industry, I have discovered that we have few jewellers in Nigeria. Most of the jewellery products we see in Nigeria are imported from other countries. Therefore, I aim to encourage the young ones to explore the jewellery industry, by igniting the creativity in them and developing their love for jewellery making. Through this programme, we will recognise and celebrate talents.
This is part of Jewellery Business Club mandate to promote the innate value in every child through its involvement in the Jewellery industry.
Topic: Catch them Young: Igniting the Creativity in your Child.
Jewellery Business Club in collaboration with Burblebespoke (one of the foremost jewellers in Nigeria) will be taking children from age 6-18 on jewellery making adventure. Selected jewelleries made by children will be displayed for sale on our platforms, and the proceeds will be sent to the Young jewellers (Their first money in jewellery Business).
